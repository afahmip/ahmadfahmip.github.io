An app where people who are very busy can hire helpers to do their daily household chores.

INTRODUCTION

Helpie! is an app where people who are very busy can hire helpers to do their daily household chores. This idea is made on purpose of joining Inkubator IT Idea Competition 2018, an idea competition held by HMIF ITB. Then we won the 1st place.

USER INTERFACE

SERVICE


Choose variety of services from us, ranging from room cleaning, lawn mowing, even babysitting. Even you can order your daily meals to us if you can't cook.


SCHEDULE


You can easily book your cleaning schedule with our helpers. Book them once and you don't have to reschedule everytime you want us to clean your room.


PACKAGE


Choose variety of packages with their own discounts and advantages.
